## 5. FAQs (formato snippet)

**`<h3>What are the best keywords for real estate ads?</h3>`**
The best real estate keywords for ads are transactional, long-tail, and hyperlocal—terms like "sell my house fast [city]," "homes for sale [neighborhood]," and "cash home buyers near me." They signal high buying or selling intent, cost less per click than broad terms, and convert far better than generic phrases like "real estate."

**`<h3>How many keywords should I use per ad group?</h3>`**
Use 10–20 tightly related keywords per ad group as a starting point, and no more than about 30. Keeping ad groups tightly themed—one intent, one location, one landing page—raises your Quality Score, lowers your cost per click, and makes your ads more relevant to each searcher.

**`<h3>What's the difference between keywords for ads and SEO keywords?</h3>`**
SEO keywords target informational searches to earn free rankings over time; ad (PPC) keywords target transactional searches you pay for per click. Ad keywords should be more specific and conversion-focused—"sell my house fast" beats "real estate market trends"—because every click costs money and you want buyers or sellers, not researchers.

**`<h3>How much do real estate ad keywords cost in %currentyear%?</h3>`**
In %currentyear%, real estate Google Ads keywords run roughly $2–$6 per click, with conversion rates near 3–3.5% and a cost per lead of about $65–$170. Seller and investor keywords usually cost more per click but are worth it given the higher value of each closed deal. Costs vary by market.

**`<h3>What are negative keywords in real estate PPC?</h3>`**
Negative keywords are terms you exclude so your ads don't show for irrelevant searches. In real estate, common negatives include "free," "cheap," "jobs," "salary," "FSBO," "classes," and "for rent" (if you don't do rentals). A strong negative list stops you from paying for clicks that will never become leads.

## 6. Notas de diferenciación

- **Ángulo "ads-first", no "SEO-first":** El top 5 del SERP (Sierra Interactive, WordStream, Luxury Presence, Placester) entrega listas pensadas para SEO on-page. Esta pieza gana porque organiza los keywords **por intención de campaña** y añade lo que un anunciante necesita de verdad: match types, ad groups y negativos.
- **Bucket investor / cash-home-buyer:** Nadie en el top 5 cubre bien los keywords de motivated-seller y cash ("we buy houses", "sell as is", "avoid foreclosure"). Es el gap más claro y encaja con el ICP de TheBaseo.
- **Datos frescos de costo (%currentyear%):** Incluir benchmarks reales de CPC/CPL da utilidad accionable que las listas puras de keywords no ofrecen, y aumenta el tiempo en página y la citabilidad en AI Overviews.
- **Negative keywords + Search Terms report:** Contenido operativo de nivel practicante que posiciona la pieza (y a Baseo) como experto real, no como agregador de listas.
