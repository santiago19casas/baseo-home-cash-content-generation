## BASEO capability hooks (Active brief)

- **Service(s) in play**: Programmatic City Pages · Technical SEO + Foundation · AI Search Optimization (AEO). (Diagnostic Audit = the CTA.)
- **WHAT + WHY hook**:
  - *City pages / custom build*: one original page per market the investor buys in, unique local copy — never swap-the-city-name templates, because Google's Scaled Content Abuse policy targets duplicated content. That's why a shared template caps you in a competitive market and a unique build doesn't.
  - *Technical/foundation*: schema markup (labels telling Google what the page is), page speed, mobile, call/form tracking as the base layer that makes the site readable and leads countable.
  - *AEO*: Q&A structure + citation-friendly schema so ChatGPT/Perplexity/AI Overviews cite the page. Track citations; recover AI traffic mislabeled "Direct" in GA4.
  - *Bi-velocity frame (CTA)*: fast wins via PPC/GBP while the organic asset compounds; paid spend shrinks as organic takes over. Qualitative only — no ROI promise.
- **Off-blog detail to avoid**: no vendor/tool names in a "we use X" frame, no per-month page/link counts, no internal QA/workflow, no BASEO package or price. The Diagnostic Audit = free, written, ~2 business days, site + competitors + market, yours to keep — state that, not the internal checklist.
