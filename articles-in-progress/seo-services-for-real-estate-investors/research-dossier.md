## BASEO capability hooks

For this Active brief:

- **Service(s) in play**: Technical SEO + Foundation · Programmatic City Pages · Seller-Situation Content · AI Search Optimization (AEO) · Editorial Backlinks + Local Authority · Lead-Focused Reporting · PPC Management · Diagnostic Audit.
- **WHAT + WHY hooks** (from `baseo-services-reference`, safe level):
  - Foundation: call/form tracking, GA4, Search Console, schema, GBP rebuild — makes leads countable and the site readable by Google, installed before content scales.
  - City pages: one original page per market the investor buys in, because Google's Scaled Content Abuse policy crushed swap-the-city-name templates; 60%+ of organic traffic lands here.
  - Situation content: pillar pages for probate, foreclosure, divorce, inherited property, problem tenants — the highest-intent long-tail; converts above average.
  - AEO: Q&A structure + citation-friendly schema so AI engines cite the client; weekly citation tracking; recover AI traffic mislabeled "Direct" in GA4.
  - Editorial backlinks: real links from chambers, REIAs, local news; no PBNs, no link farms.
  - Lead reporting: leads with phone numbers, cost-per-lead trend, pages published, AI citations, next month's plan in plain English; leads scored by intent.
  - PPC: motivated-seller campaigns under the same lead/cost-per-deal lens; shrinks as organic compounds (most cut 30–50% by months 6–9).
  - Audit: free written diagnostic in ~2 business days, 3 biggest issues + competitor gap + deal math, yours to keep, no call required. Honest broker: if the market doesn't support the math, the audit says so.
- **Off-blog detail to avoid**: call-tracking vendor names, exact per-month page/link counts, internal QA/approval workflow, citation-tracking tooling, report template/layout, link-outreach methodology, account structure/bid cadence for PPC. Keep every service at WHAT/WHY/that-we-do-it.
- **Cross-service facts (safe anywhere)**: month-to-month, 30 days' notice; written monthly SOW with refund if a deliverable is missed; market exclusivity per metro; client owns all content/links/data/reports; no ranking guarantees ever.
