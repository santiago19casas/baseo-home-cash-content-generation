## External authoritative sources

### Tier 1 (primary docs & data)
- [HUD HomeStore](https://www.hudhomestore.gov/) — official HUD REO marketplace; owner-occupant priority window; bids via HUD-registered broker.
- [Fannie Mae HomePath](https://www.homepath.fanniemae.com/) — Fannie Mae REO; "First Look" owner-occupant window; free search.
- [Freddie Mac HomeSteps](https://www.homesteps.com/) — Freddie Mac REO; First Look Initiative.
- [USDA REO & Foreclosure Properties (resales.usda.gov)](https://www.resales.usda.gov/resales/public/home) — official USDA rural REO/resale listings; free.
