### H2: The Special Ad Category: What Changed and Why It Matters

- **Word target**: 180–230 (parent) + H3s
- **Sub-bullets**:
  1. Plain-language the rule: because "we buy houses" ads are about housing, Meta forces them into the Housing Special Ad Category, which strips most targeting. Source: [Meta Business Help](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1198401317374558).
  2. Why: HUD's 2022 settlement over discriminatory housing targeting ([Meta Newsroom](https://about.fb.com/news/2022/06/expanding-our-work-on-ads-fairness/)).
  3. Why it matters more to a cash buyer than an agent: your whole hook is geographic and situational, and both are now constrained.
- **External links**: Meta Business Help + Meta Newsroom.
- **BASEO reference**: none.
- **Visual element**: none (H3s carry it).
- **Snippet capture**: no.
