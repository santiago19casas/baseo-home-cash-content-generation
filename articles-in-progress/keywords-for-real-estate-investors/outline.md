### H2: Why keyword strategy is different for investors than for agents

- **Word target**: 140–170
- **Sub-bullets**:
  1. Take the stance: agent keywords are a trap for a cash buyer. Agents chase buyer-side terms ("homes for sale in [city]," "best real estate agent"); you chase seller-side, high-urgency terms ("sell my house fast," "cash for houses").
  2. Different searcher, different money: an agent wants a buyer browsing; you want a distressed seller ready to deal this week.
  3. Portals (Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin) own the generic head terms. You will not outrank them, and half that traffic isn't a seller anyway.
  4. So investors win on long-tail + local, and build dedicated pages for niches: distressed, probate, pre-foreclosure.
  5. Data: investor SEO targets motivated/distressed sellers, not "homes for sale."
