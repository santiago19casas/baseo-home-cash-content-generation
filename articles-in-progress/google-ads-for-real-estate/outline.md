### H2: The Housing Special Ad Category: what breaks most accounts

- **Word target**: 120–160
- **Sub-bullets**:
  1. Google classifies real estate as a Special Ad Category (housing) in the US and Canada. Data: [Google Ads Help](https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/16701755?hl=en).

  3. Ignoring it: disapprovals, limited delivery, or account restriction that's hard to reverse.
  4. Frame as the #1 mistake new real estate advertisers make. Niche note via [Carrot](https://carrot.com/blog/google-ads-housing-policy/).
