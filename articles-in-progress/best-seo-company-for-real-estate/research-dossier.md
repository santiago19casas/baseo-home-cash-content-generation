## BASEO capability hooks

For Active brief:
- **Service(s) in play**: Diagnostic Audit, Technical SEO + Foundation, Programmatic City Pages, Seller-Situation Content, AI Search Optimization (AEO), Editorial Backlinks + Local Authority, Lead-Focused Reporting, PPC Management.
- **WHAT + WHY hooks** (safe level from `baseo-services-reference`):
  - Diagnostic Audit: free written audit (3 biggest issues, competitor keyword gap, deal-math projection) in ~2 business days; yours to keep; honest-broker (says no if the market doesn't support the math). WHY: risk reversal for a burned buyer.
  - Technical Foundation: install call/form tracking, GA4, GSC, schema, GBP rebuild first. WHY: leads become countable before content scales.
  - City Pages: one original page per market, never templates. WHY: Scaled Content Abuse policy crushed swap-the-city-name pages; 60%+ of organic traffic lands on city pages.
  - Situation Content: probate, foreclosure, divorce, inherited, problem tenants. WHY: highest-intent, above-average conversion.
  - AEO: Q&A formatting + citation-friendly schema; weekly AI-citation tracking; recover AI traffic mislabeled "Direct." WHY: AI search sends high-intent leads.
  - Editorial Backlinks: chambers, REIAs, local news; no PBNs/farms. WHY: authority that moves rankings.
  - Lead-Focused Reporting: leads with phone numbers, CPL trend, pages published, AI citations, next month's plan. WHY: the accountability the last agency skipped.
  - PPC Management: motivated-seller campaigns under the same CPL/cost-per-deal lens; shrinks 30–50% by months 6–9 as organic compounds. WHY: hybrid honesty.
- **Off-blog detail to avoid**: call-tracking vendor and tool stack; exact pages-per-month / links-per-month counts; internal QA/approval workflow; report template/layout; link-outreach methodology; lead-scoring model; PPC account structure. Cross-service safe facts: month-to-month + 30 days' notice; written monthly SOW with refund if a deliverable is missed; market exclusivity per metro; client owns all content/links/data.
