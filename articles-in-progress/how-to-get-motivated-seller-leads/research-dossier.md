- [Real Estate Bees — Average Wholesale Assignment Fee (survey)](https://realestatebees.com/statistics/average-wholesale-assignment-fee/) — national average **$13,000**; typical range $5,000–$20,000; beginners $2,000–$5,000. Deal-math anchor.

### Tier 3 (practitioners)
- [Verse.ai — Speed-to-Lead Statistics](https://verse.ai/blog/speed-to-lead-statistics) — responding within 5 minutes → up to 21× more likely to qualify a lead; contact-rate collapses after minutes. Speed-to-lead / follow-up section.

- [Realty Crux — Google PPC Ads for Real Estate Investors](https://realtycrux.com/google-ppc-ads-for-real-estate-investors/) — "sell house fast" paid clicks run ~**$12–$63 per click**; seller CPL ~$100–$150+, up to ~$350 in tough metros. Paid-search math.
