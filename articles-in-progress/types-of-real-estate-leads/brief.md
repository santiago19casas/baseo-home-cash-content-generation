## Angle / POV

Most "types of real estate leads" articles are flat lists that treat every lead type as equal. This one classifies leads across two axes (WHO they are: buyer vs seller; HOW they arrive: inbound vs outbound), adds a temperature layer, then ranks the sources by real 2026 cost per lead and conversion so the reader knows what to actually chase. The BASEO tilt: cost per lead is a trap metric. Portal leads look cheap-ish but convert at 0.4–1.2%; referrals and inbound organic leads cost near-zero and convert 10–25x higher. Intent and speed-to-lead drive profit, not a low CPL. That reframe makes the case for owning an inbound channel obvious on its own merits.
