#### H3: 1. Public Records & County Filings (Notice of Default)
- Free leads from county recorder/title company: NOD, Lis Pendens, trustee sale dates. Title companies sometimes share lists with partner agents. Pro: free, fresh at source. Con: manual, needs skip tracing. Tip: filings 14–60 days old. Keywords: notice of default, lis pendens, public records.

#### H3: 2. The MLS and Portals (Zillow, Realtor.com, RealtyTrac)
- Filter already-listed short sales on MLS/portals; useful buyer-side and to catch expired/withdrawn listings to recapture. Con: already listed = another agent has it; better as market/comps source than virgin leads. Cross-reference expired listings.

#### H3: 3. Short Sale Data Lists & Lead Providers
- Data/prospecting services (REDX/Vortex, PropStream, PropertyRadar, Landvoice, Vulcan7) deliver mortgage balance, delinquency, equity, contact data, daily-updated across many areas. Neutral examples, not affiliate. Pro: scale + freshness. Con: recurring cost + every agent buys the same list, so speed/outreach quality wins. Keyword: short sale lead sources.

#### H3: 4. Direct Mail to Pre-Foreclosure Homeowners
- Empathetic (not predatory) letters/postcards to pre-foreclosure lists. Soft message, repeated sequence (not one-and-done), clear CTA. Cite PropertyRadar preforeclosure marketing do's/don'ts. Typical response 0.5–2% → volume + follow-up game.

#### H3: 5. Live Lead Transfer Services
- Define live lead transfer: service generates, pre-qualifies, and warm-transfers a seller by live call. Pro: immediate contact, higher conversion than a cold list. Con: higher cost per lead, variable quality. Ask for a trial; measure cost-per-listing, not cost-per-lead.

#### H3: 6. Referral Networks (Attorneys, Lenders, Probate Pros)
- Bankruptcy/foreclosure attorneys, lender loss-mitigation, probate pros know first. Build via referral reciprocity, being the trusted distressed-case agent, trade orgs/local communities. Highest-quality, lowest-cost, slow to build. Keyword: referral network, loss mitigation.

#### H3: 7. Cold Calling & Skip Tracing Distressed Owners
- Combine a pre-foreclosure list with skip tracing (REDX, PropStream) for phones and call. Include a 2–3 line empathetic opener. Rules: DNC scrubbing, legal hours, compassionate tone. Link to compliance section. Con: high rejection, needs volume + thick skin.
