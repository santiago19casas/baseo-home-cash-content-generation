## Angle / POV

The take that separates this from every generic "real estate leads" listicle: leads are not a monolith, they are a motivation spectrum on the acquisition side and a disposition problem on the buyer side, and the real strategic decision is not *which vendor* but *rent vs own*. Every lead you buy is a lead a competitor can also buy; a page that ranks for "sell my house fast [city]" is exclusive inbound you own 24/7. The smart play is bi-velocity: paid channels (PPC/LSA/GBP) for leads this week while SEO/AEO compounds into a channel that can't be outbid. Deal math over adjectives, no ROI promise, honest that paid buys speed and owned buys durability.
